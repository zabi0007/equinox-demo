Tour Overview

An unforgettable ride through the northern mountains

The Northern Valleys Expedition is designed for riders who want more than a standard trail ride. This journey combines mountain scenery, authentic local atmosphere, carefully selected horses, and a route built for adventure.

Over the course of 8 days, riders pass through open valleys, mountain tracks, panoramic ridgelines, and remote camp locations that create a truly memorable horseback experience.

The tour is ideal for riders with a reasonable level of confidence in the saddle and a desire to explore dramatic natural landscapes in a more immersive way.
